Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of any data associated with your use of the TruePulse app, please follow the instructions below:

Send an email to jftsabondo@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

Include the following in your message:

  • Your full name
  • Your Facebook or Instagram user ID (if applicable)
  • Any other relevant information to identify your data

We will process your request within 7 days.
